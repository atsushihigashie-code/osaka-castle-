Where Hideyori and Yodo-dono Fell

In this quiet corner, the final memory of the Summer Siege of Osaka — 1615 — is written into the ground. Toyotomi Hideyori, son and heir of Hideyoshi, and his mother Yodo-dono, were cornered here by Tokugawa forces. They took their own lives on this spot. The story of the Toyotomi family, and of the original, glittering Osaka Castle, comes to an end right here.

The castle keep you see today was actually rebuilt entirely from scratch by the Tokugawa afterward. The Toyotomi-era castle lies buried beneath what you're standing on now.
