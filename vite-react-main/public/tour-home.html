Before You Begin

Two Castles, One Ground

What you're about to walk through isn't one castle's story — it's two. Hideyoshi's original castle was burned in 1615 and buried; the Tokugawa built an entirely different castle directly on top of it. Every wall you'll see sits on that hidden layer.
